HOW IT WORKS

Simple. Legal. Exciting.

Choose how many tickets you want. Each ticket costs as little as ₹49. The more tickets you hold, the higher your odds of winning. Bundle deals available.

Pay securely via UPI, Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, or Wallets powered by Razorpay. All transactions are encrypted and RBI compliant.

Winners are contacted within 24 hours. Prizes are delivered or arranged within 30 days. All taxes (TDS as per Income Tax Act) are handled transparently.

🆓 FREE ENTRY OPTION: Under the Prize Competition Act, no purchase is necessary to enter. Send your name, address, phone number, and the answer to the skill question on a postcard to: Alpha Competitions Pvt. Ltd., 12th Floor, One BKC, Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai - 400051.

ODDS OF WINNING

Your odds depend on the number of tickets issued per competition and how many you purchase. If a competition has 10,000 total tickets and you buy 10, your odds are 10 in 10,000 (1 in 1,000). Odds are always displayed on each competition page.

TAXES

Prize winnings in India are subject to TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) at 30% under Section 194B of the Income Tax Act for prizes exceeding ₹10,000. TDS is deducted at source before prize delivery. Winners receive a TDS certificate (Form 16A) from Alpha.

LEGALITY

Alpha operates as a skill-based competition platform, not a lottery. Our model complies with the Public Gambling Act 1867, the Information Technology Act 2000, the Consumer Protection Act 2019, and all applicable state laws. We hold all required licenses and registrations.

Last Updated: January 1, 2024 | Effective: January 1, 2024
This Privacy Policy describes how Alpha Competition Pvt. Ltd. ("Alpha", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services. This policy complies with the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and the Personal Data Protection framework applicable in India.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, mobile number, date of birth, password
  • Identity Verification (KYC): PAN card, Aadhaar number (masked), government-issued photo ID for prize winners
  • Payment Information: UPI ID, bank account details (processed via Razorpay; we do not store card numbers)
  • Contact Details: Delivery address, city, state, PIN code
  • Communications: Support queries, feedback, complaints

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device identifier
  • Browsing activity on our platform (pages visited, competition entries)
  • Transaction logs, entry records, ticket purchase history
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Payment verification data from Razorpay
  • Social login data (if you use Google/Facebook login)
  • Fraud prevention data from third-party services

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process competition entries and payments
  • To conduct draws and notify winners
  • To verify your identity (KYC) for prize delivery
  • To comply with TDS obligations under the Income Tax Act
  • To send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide competition services you've signed up for
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional data sharing
  • Legal Obligation: KYC, TDS compliance, PMLA compliance
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud prevention, service improvement

4. DATA SHARING

We share your data with:

  • Razorpay Financial Solutions Pvt. Ltd. — Payment processing
  • MSG91 / TextLocal — SMS notifications
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — Cloud hosting

  • Law Enforcement — When legally required

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. DATA RETENTION

  • Account data: Retained for 5 years after account closure (as required by Indian law)
  • Transaction records: 7 years (GST and accounting requirements)
  • KYC documents: 5 years post-transaction (PMLA requirements)
  • Marketing data: Until consent is withdrawn

6. YOUR RIGHTS

Under applicable Indian law and our policy, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing
  • Opt out of profiling
  • Raise a grievance with our Data Protection Officer

7. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures including AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.3 for data transmission, secure server infrastructure with ISO 27001 certification, regular security audits, and restricted employee access on a need-to-know basis.
